Features

Everything you need to
master your routine

📊

Drift Analysis

Automatically calculates the gap between your planned and actual time for every task, giving you a real drift score each day.

🧠

Smart Insights

AI-powered suggestions based on your logged activity patterns to help you identify your most productive hours and habits.

📅

Routine Planner

Add planned routines with start time, end time, and priority. Log actuals later to compare your plan vs reality.

📈

Visual Charts

Routine comparison bar charts, productive vs wasted pie charts, and drift trend line graphs — all in one dashboard.

🔔

Notifications

Get drift alerts, daily productivity summaries, and task reminders to keep you on track throughout the day.

📄

Export & History

Export your data as JSON or PDF. Browse your full activity history and clear data to start fresh anytime.

How It Works

Simple 3-step system

1

Plan Your Day

Add your planned routines for the day with task name, start time, end time, and priority level.

2

Log Actuals

After completing each task, log the actual time spent. HABITFLUX computes your drift score instantly.

3

Analyze & Improve

Review charts, read AI smart suggestions, and use your drift data to build better habits over time.
